## Retrieval Strategies

- **Quick switcher** (`Cmd/Ctrl+O`): Search by title or content
- **Global search** (`Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+F`): Full-text across vault
- **Graph view**: Visualize connections between notes
- **Backlinks**: See what notes reference the current one
- **Tags**: `#tag` for cross-cutting topics
- **Links**: `[[wiki-link]]` for intentional relationships
