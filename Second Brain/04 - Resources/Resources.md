# Resources

Reference material — things you've collected from the outside world.

## Subfolders

- **Articles** — Saved articles and essays
- **Books** — Book notes and highlights
- **Courses** — Course materials and certificates
- **People** — Notes about people you know or follow
- **Tools** — Software, apps, and utilities you use
- **Web** — Clipped web pages and bookmarks

## Convention

- Resources are external knowledge you've consumed — not your own ideas
- Use the Literature Note template from `90 - Templates/` for captured content
- Always link the source (URL, book ISBN, etc.) in frontmatter
- Tag by topic so you can cross-reference with [[05 - Knowledge/Knowledge|Knowledge]]

## Rules

1. Capture the source, not just the content
2. Add your own thoughts — don't just copy-paste
3. If a resource sparks a permanent idea, create a [[05 - Knowledge/Knowledge|Permanent Note]] and link back here
4. Use the Obsidian Web Clipper or manual paste with source attribution
