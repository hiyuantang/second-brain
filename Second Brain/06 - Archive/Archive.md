# Archive

Completed projects, outdated references, and inactive items.

## Convention

- Items are moved here, not deleted
- Keep the original structure when archiving a project folder
- Tag archived items with `#status/archived`
- You can always search and retrieve from the archive

## What Goes Here

- Completed [[02 - Projects/Projects|Projects]]
- Superseded [[04 - Resources/Resources|Resources]]
- Outdated [[03 - Areas/Areas|Areas]]
- Old [[01 - Daily/Daily|Daily]] notes (optional — keep recent ones accessible)

## Rules

1. Archive, don't delete — you may need it later
2. Add a note explaining why it was archived and when
3. Archived items are still searchable and linked
